My work

Villi yrttiseikkailu

First year studies mobile game project, which was done for a client. My main roles were a scrummaster and a graphic designer. I took the main responsibility of the communication with the client and between the team. I also managed the schedules and product backlog of the project. On the graphic side, I designed and drew all the plants, scenery, items, menu icons and buttons. The graphics are made using Gimp as 2D graphics and we animated them in Unity. The game is programmed in C# and published on Google Play. Our project team's game won 2022 year annual vote as the best game of the course.
